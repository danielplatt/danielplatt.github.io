I'm a postdoc at Imperial College London with a research interest in geometric analysis and machine learning. I previously was a postdoc at King's College London and obtained my PhD from Imperial College London where my advisors were Jason Lotay (University of Oxford) and Sir Simon Donaldson (Imperial College). My postdoc supervisors are Richard Thomas and Toby Wiseman. My Full Academic CV can be downloaded here.
